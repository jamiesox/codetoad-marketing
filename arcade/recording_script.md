# CodeToad Arcade Recording Script

**Objective:** Create a ~60-second, high-energy product demo video showcasing CodeToad's core value proposition: scan, analyze, and autonomously fix a codebase.

**Style:** Fast-paced, engaging, with clear on-screen text overlays and a confident, expert voiceover.

---

### **Scene 1: The Hook (0-5s)**

*   **Visual:** Start on the CodeToad landing page. Fast zoom into the headline: "Your Codebase, Production-Ready".
*   **Narration:** "Is your codebase *really* production-ready? CodeToad uses AI to find and fix bugs before they ever hit production."
*   **On-screen Text:** Your Codebase, Production-Ready.

---

### **Scene 2: The Scan (5-15s)**

*   **Visual:** Transition to the Sign In screen. Click "Sign in with GitHub". Jump to the Dashboard, showing several repositories. Click the "Import Repository" button. Show the import modal, select a repo, and click "Import".
*   **Narration:** "Just connect your GitHub repo. CodeToad performs a deep scan, analyzing every file for bugs, incomplete features, and security vulnerabilities."
*   **On-screen Text:** 1. Connect Your Repo. 2. Deep Code Analysis.

---

### **Scene 3: The Health Report (15-30s)**

*   **Visual:** Jump to the Health Report for the "super-spork" repository. Pan across the Health Score ring (85/100). Show the Issue Distribution chart, highlighting the "Bugs" and "Incomplete" bars. Scroll down to the 9 quality dimension metrics.
*   **Narration:** "In minutes, you get a detailed health report. We score your codebase across nine quality dimensions and pinpoint every issue, from critical bugs to unfinished pseudo-code."
*   **On-screen Text:** Instant Health Score. Find Every Issue.

---

### **Scene 4: The AI Workspace (30-45s)**

*   **Visual:** Click the "Analyze & Fix" button. Transition to the AI Workspace. Show the file tree on the left, the code editor in the middle, and the AI Chat on the right. A bug is highlighted in the code.
*   **Narration:** "This is the AI Workspace. On the left, our agent has already queued up every task. In the middle, the editor shows the exact file and line with the bug."
*   **On-screen Text:** AI-Powered Workspace. Bugs Identified.

---

### **Scene 5: The AI Chat & Fix (45-55s)**

*   **Visual:** In the AI Chat panel, a user types: "What are the main bugs in this codebase?" The AI instantly responds with a detailed explanation and a one-line code fix. The user clicks "Start Agent".
*   **Narration:** "You can chat with the AI to understand the issues, or just click 'Start Agent' and let our autonomous fixer handle everything. It writes the code, runs the tests, and prepares a pull request."
*   **On-screen Text:** Chat with AI. Autonomous Fixes.

---

### **Scene 6: The CTA (55-60s)**

*   **Visual:** End on the Pricing page, with the "Free Scan" and "Fix Plans" cards visible. The mouse cursor moves to click the "Scan Your Repo Free" button.
*   **Narration:** "Stop shipping bugs. Get your free codebase scan at CodeToad.io."
*   **On-screen Text:** Get Your Free Scan. CodeToad.io.
